SHARED CONTINUITY PREFIX
Continue the exact same illustrated shot from the supplied Beat 1 master, not a

BEAT 2 - SHADOW STIRS, CREATURE UNSEEN
Advance only a few seconds. Keep the witness's feet and grounded staff nearly
fixed. He turns his eyes and shoulders a few degrees toward the diagonal
boundary, grip tightening, robe edge lifting slightly: first conscious unease,
not panic. At the exact sun-shadow boundary beside the right arcade, show only
a subtle low disturbance: dust curling against its normal fall, loose grit
shifting, the edge of the shadow seeming to ripple inward. Qeteb remains
completely unseen: no scale, hair, eye, silhouette, body, limb, or face. Preserve
negative space and the exact locked geometry.

BEAT 3 - ENTRY ACROSS THE LINE
Advance one continuous action beat. From deep right arcade shadow, Qeteb rolls
low into view along and across the precise diagonal noon boundary, roughly one
third of its mass visible. Required anatomy: a non-humanoid rolling amorphous
mound made of many irregular overlapping scales interwoven with coarse shaggy
hair; no head, neck, torso, arms, or legs. Exactly one unblinking eye is embedded
on the forward body mass over its cardiac center, not on a face: pale sepia iris
with a dark vertical reptilian slit, no glow and no other eyes. The sun-shadow
line bisects the tumbling mass so scales catch hard noon highlights while hair
disappears into black shade. Witness makes one small recoil toward frame-left,
weight shifting back, staff still in anatomical right hand, dread beginning.
Same wide camera.

BEAT 4 - FACE-TO-EYE CONFRONTATION
Advance the same rolling motion without a cut. Qeteb has moved closer along the
unchanged diagonal boundary and now occupies the right-center foreground, still
a low amorphous rolling mass of overlapping scales and shaggy hair, never
upright. Keep exactly one chest-centered unblinking slit eye; rotate the tumbling
body just enough that this eye fixes directly on the witness. Witness remains
the star and fully recognizable, now turned toward it, leaning back with bent
knees, left hand raised defensively, right hand bracing the same crooked staff
diagonally across his body. Maintain separation between figures, the visible
noon sun, and enough architecture to prove the camera never moved. Maximum
dread, no contact yet, no violence.

BEAT 5 - AMBIGUOUS AFTERMATH
Advance after an unseen instant, same camera and setting. The identical witness
lies prone but non-graphically on the sunlit stones near his prior position,
body angled along the diagonal boundary; robe, headcloth, sash, and sandals
unchanged; crooked staff fallen nearby and clearly the same object. No blood,
wound, gore, or explicit cause. Qeteb is rolling away into the deep right arcade
shadow along the same boundary, its rear mass still visible and anatomically
consistent: overlapping scales plus shaggy hair, no limbs or head; the one chest
eye is seen only in oblique profile or turning away, with no second eye. Dust
settles across the line. Noon sun remains harsh and indifferent; what happened
is unresolved.

read as shaggy hair); no multiple eyes, facial eye, floating eye, giant eyeball
